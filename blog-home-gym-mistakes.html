Top 3 Home Gym Mistakes I Made

Creating a home gym sounds like a dream — no commute, no crowds, no excuses. But I made a few rookie mistakes early on that slowed my progress. Here’s what I learned so you don’t have to.

1. Buying Too Much, Too Fast

I got excited and ordered all the gear: weights, resistance bands, a stepper, a yoga mat… and most of it sat untouched for months. Start small. Use it consistently. Then upgrade.

2. No Dedicated Space

For a while, I worked out wherever there was room — bedroom, basement, laundry area. I finally cleared one small space just for workouts. Game changer. Your space = your mindset.

3. Skipping the Schedule

I thought “working out at home” meant I’d just squeeze it in. Nope. If it’s not scheduled, it’s not happening. Now I treat it like an appointment — even if it’s just 10 minutes.

If you're starting or restarting your home fitness journey, know this: you don’t need perfect gear or perfect timing. Just start where you are — and don’t make these mistakes 😉
